Perennial Big Brother Addiction

Perennial Big Brother Addiction or PBBA is a real disease affecting 1 or 2 people annually in the U.S. Educate yourself on PBBA so that you can help those in need, and alleviate symptoms if they are on the early onset stages.

Basic Information

PBBA stands for Perennial Big Brother Addiction and is one of the least common reality tv disorders affecting only 1 or 2 people annually in the U.S. Up until 2018 it was frequently misdiagnosed as Superfandom. Adults with PBBA may have trouble being themselves around others, particularly individuals involved with the production of BB. There is an impulsive behavior to do whatever it takes to get their next BB fix sometimes at the cost of close personal relationships.

Signs & Symptoms

It is normal for BB viewers to fantasize about the show form time to time and daydream about being on the show. However, viewers with PBBA experience an onset of other symptoms that may get more aggressive over time if not diagnosed and treated properly.

An adult with PBBA might experience:
  • Superfan Migraines
  • ZingBot Paranoia
  • Hearing Don Wollman voices in their head
  • Mental Confusion
  • Racing Thoughts
  • BB Sweats
  • Anxiety Farts
  • Outbursts During Diary Room Sessions and/or Live Evictions

Treatment

Currently, as of 2018, there is no cure for PBBA. However, BB addicts can learn to cope and live with their disease by following some or all of the steps below.

  • Talk to someone
  • Watch old seasons during the months of Oct - May
  • Attempt to watch international versions of BB
  • Set up Google alerts to be notified of anything BB related to get quick fixes throughout the 9-5 workday
  • Apply to be on BB
  • Re-apply to get on BB
  • Repeat the previous step 9 times
  • Get on BB
  • Don’t get evicted week one
  • Continue previous steps until finale night
  • Accept your disease & live your life

Superfans vs PBBA

Because PBBA can easily be misdiagnosed as Superfandom we’ve outlined key differences below:

Superfans PBBA
❌ Superfans are braggadocious. They are not shy about BB and tell anyone that will give them attention for two minutes ✔️ Addicts typically live their life in a closet, closed off from the world and are embarrassed by their disease
❌ Superfans watch the live feeds ✔️ Addicts wait for the pure cut primetime & live shows
❌ Superfans are quick to critique/ridicule BB ✔️ The show is like sex to Addicts - they only complain when they aren't getting any
❌ Superfans name pets after the show ✔️ Addicts name their children after the show
❌ Due to Superfans passive lifestyle they lack life experience and thus only talk about past houseguests and past seasons ✔️ Addicts are full of life experiences and therefore have actual stories to share
